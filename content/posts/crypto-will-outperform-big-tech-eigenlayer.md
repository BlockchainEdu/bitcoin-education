We talked to Ishaan from EigenLayer to uncover his journey into crypto during COVID - and how it changed everything.

From using stablecoins to save small businesses to pioneering new tech like ZK proofs and trusted execution environments, Ishaan reveals how he transitioned through the blockchain world - from AVAX to Cosmos and now Ethereum.

## What we covered

- COVID Crypto Awakening: How the pandemic drove a new wave of builders into the blockchain space.
- Stablecoins for Real Business: Using stablecoins to help small businesses survive during economic uncertainty.
- Actively Validated Services (AVSs): EigenLayer's new approach to extending Ethereum's security to new protocols.
- Restaking Revolution: How restaking is unlocking new possibilities for Ethereum validators and the broader ecosystem.
