We are proud to announce the launch of the first ever Intercollegiate Crypto Fund!

Our goal with the BEN Crypto Fund is to empower our members to showcase their Fundamental &amp; Technical prowess without the need for them to put up any capital risk.

Initially, to garner interest, we created a BEN Crypto Fund Committee to gauge the sentiment on who/whom would be intrigued to act as the Fund’s Lead Analyst(s). As a result, we created a relatively simple application in which committee members had to submit a thorough methodology thesis that had to answer the following:

- **Strategy Memo**- **Token Inclusion Criteria**- **Associated Fees**- **Maintenance Threshold(s)**- **Qualifications/Experience**- **Suggested Platform to Launch**
In turn, we received a flood of proficient applicants who delivered innovative Fund strategies from both ends of the risk spectrum.

Ultimately, we selected 4 well-versed Fund Analysts representing **Rutgers University, Chapman University, Boston College, and Wilfred Laurier University!**

We have equipped our Fund Analysts with Messari Premium accounts to further assist them in creating comprehensive and in-depth analyses for their proposals.

Our Fund Analyst application is open on a rolling basis, so please feel free to check it out [here](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1syjhX-yNATNMG0jO2KFXB-pBif5eatDY6VePWQLqkZM/edit?usp=sharing).
