What happens when an experienced sales leader turns his frustration with revenue planning into a SaaS startup?

Adam Kling, CEO of Territories.ai, shares how he went from a secure insurance career to launching a company that's solving one of the toughest challenges in startups. Learn how he took an 80% pay cut, embraced risk, and used AI to create smarter revenue plans while balancing life as an entrepreneur.

## What we covered

- Taking the Leap: Adam took an 80% pay cut to pursue his startup vision - and why he'd do it again.
- AI-Powered Revenue Planning: How Territories.ai uses artificial intelligence to help startups create smarter, more efficient revenue plans.
- Bootstrapping Reality: Practical advice on managing finances and expectations when building a startup from scratch.
- From Sales to SaaS: The transferable skills that made a sales career the perfect foundation for building a tech company.
