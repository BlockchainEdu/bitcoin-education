With Chris Buren from Galaxy Digital, we get into the wild world of crypto trading and how he went from a college student at Rutgers to leading the charge at one of the biggest crypto firms in the world.

## What we covered

- From Campus to Crypto: Chris shares how he discovered crypto while at Rutgers University and turned his passion into a career at Galaxy Digital.
- Trading Perpetuals: Learn the fundamentals of trading perpetual contracts and how institutional traders approach the crypto markets.
- Hedging Strategies: Professional risk management techniques that protect portfolios during volatile market conditions.
- Staying Ahead: Insights on how to remain competitive in one of the fastest-moving industries on the planet.
