We talked to Ari Santos from AXAL to discuss how they're changing the game with smart on-chain automation using autonomous agents.

We break down AXAL's autopilot trading bot, which handles everything from portfolio rebalancing and yield harvesting to setting stops, limits, and protecting your wallet from market swings - all tailored to your risk profile.

## What we covered

- Autonomous On-Chain Agents: AXAL uses AI-powered agents to automate complex crypto trading strategies, removing the need for constant manual monitoring.
- Portfolio Rebalancing: The autopilot bot dynamically adjusts your portfolio based on market conditions and your personal risk tolerance.
- Yield Harvesting: Automatically captures yield opportunities across DeFi protocols without requiring users to manually move funds.
- Risk Management: Built-in stop-loss, limit orders, and wallet protection features help safeguard against sudden market downturns.
