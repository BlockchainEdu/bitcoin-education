What does it mean to decentralize talent data? How are builder scores and brain data reshaping hiring, airdrops, and tokenomics?

Pedro Oliveira shares how Talent Protocol is changing talent sourcing in Web3, from launching the Builder Score to embracing transparency and AI-driven tools.

## What we covered

- Builder Score: A reputation metric that quantifies a person's contributions to the Web3 ecosystem, used for hiring and airdrop eligibility.
- Decentralized Talent Data: Moving professional identity data away from centralized platforms like LinkedIn.
- AI-Driven Talent Tools: How artificial intelligence is helping match talent with opportunities in the blockchain space.
- Challenging LinkedIn: Why Web3 needs its own professional network built on transparency and verifiable credentials.
