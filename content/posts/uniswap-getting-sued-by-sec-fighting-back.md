## Uniswap Labs Receives Wells Notice from SEC

Uniswap Labs has received a Wells notice from the SEC, signaling potential enforcement action. Despite this challenge, the company plans to continue developing its products.

### Key Arguments from Uniswap:

- Most tokens aren't securities but rather new forms of digital value
- These tokens enable innovative economic activities that don't fit traditional securities frameworks
- Current regulatory frameworks are outdated and need updating
- Uniswap is fighting to keep their innovative, open tech available for all
