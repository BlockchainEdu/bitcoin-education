We explore Vlad's journey from mathematician and Motorola engineer to CEO of Algebra.

Learn how he overcame early challenges, pivoted to power over 50 DEXs, and developed new DeFi solutions like dynamic fees, built-in farming, and customizable liquidity layers.

## What we covered

- Engineer to CEO: Vlad's transition from working at Motorola to building one of DeFi's most important infrastructure companies.
- Powering 50+ DEXs: How Algebra Finance became the backend for dozens of decentralized exchanges across multiple chains.
- Dynamic Fees: An innovative fee mechanism that automatically adjusts based on market conditions and trading volume.
- Customizable Liquidity: Algebra's liquidity layers allow DEX operators to tailor their AMM behavior to specific use cases.
