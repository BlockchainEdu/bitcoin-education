Join Antonio, president of the Blockchain Education Network (BEN), as he takes you through an inspiring journey of how one determined student, Thomas, transformed his life with the help of BEN's mission and opportunities in the crypto space.

From Istanbul to Lisbon, follow Thomas as he attends life-changing conferences, builds connections, and lands his dream job - thanks to the power of networking.

## What we covered

- Student Success Story: Thomas went from being a student in Istanbul to landing his dream job in crypto through BEN's network.
- Conference Access: BEN provides free conference tickets, accommodation, and educational resources to students worldwide.
- Networking Power: How attending the right events and connecting with the right people can transform a career.
- BEN's Mission: Supporting the next generation of blockchain leaders through education, mentorship, and real-world opportunities.
