We talked to Joaquim and Vaughn from Alpaca Network to dive into the exciting intersection of AI and crypto.

From early Bitcoin investments to the rise of Initial Model Offerings (IMOs), we look at how Alpaca Network is working to democratize AI and empower developers through decentralization.

## What we covered

- Initial Model Offerings (IMOs): A new fundraising mechanism that tokenizes AI models, allowing communities to invest in and benefit from AI development.
- Decentralized AI: Alpaca Network aims to create an open AI coordination layer where developers can build, share, and monetize AI models without centralized gatekeepers.
- The PACA Token: Powers the Alpaca Network ecosystem, enabling governance, staking, and access to AI resources.
- Crypto Origins: Both founders share their personal journeys into crypto and how early investments shaped their vision.
